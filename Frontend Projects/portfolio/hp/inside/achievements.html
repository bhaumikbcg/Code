Won 1st Prize for the best use of Dolby's API and best UI design

  • The sound quality was enhanced for better clarity and to introduce sorround sound in web pages containing educational videos and audios

  • The technologies used were HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, JQuery, Bootstrap & Dolby API
  • The website was basically made for my friend who had her birthday coming up, for dolby who also had it's 50th year in the industry and for uploading educational videos like coursera, but here the difference would be that the audio would be in sorround sound due to which children will pay 30% more attention
