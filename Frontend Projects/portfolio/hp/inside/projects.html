Intelligent Shopping Cart

Electronics & Embedded Systems

The concept in making this project was to think of the Future Shopping Stores & Malls, where we could ease the customer’s time and efforts to buy their desired items. The idea was to replace the conventional Barcode System, which is used today in most of the Stores. In this, the shopping cart will be fixed with a Micro – Controller Device, RFID Receiver and a Display, that shows the complete map of a store with areas demarking where and which products are available.
Every Product will be having its own RFID Tag, which will automatically send signal to the RFID Receiver on the cart as it comes in its dynamic range. When the item is placed in the cart, the Micro controller and the Sensors on the cart automatically calculates the total amount payable by the customer.
An Embedded C code was developed for communication between Micro controller and the RFID and the look of the screen was defined using Visual Basic.

Speed Control of Stepper Motor

Electronics & Embedded Systems

An Embedded System was used and it was interfaced with a LCD screen, a HEX keypad and a stepper motor.
The four modes of operation were Fast Clockwise, Fast Anti Clockwise, Slow Clockwise, Slow Anti Clockwise.
An Embedded C code was developed for communication between the controller, the LCD, the Motor and the HEX Keypad, and was loaded into the Micro Controller ATMEGA 16
