About Us

Yumvino is the premiere wine tasting website and app for people to search, enjoy, and review wineries all over the United States. Whether it is for a romantic weekend getaway, a vacation with the girls, or a fun afternoon with friends, Yumvino is always striving to connect great people to great wine. With world class customer service and enthusiastic users, we will continue to bring the best and most current winery information to all wine lovers.
